Policies

  • Advanced deposits - A deposit is required to confirm all reservations. Deposits are due two (2) days from date of reservation.
  • Deposits require: (1 night FULL RATE) - (2-13) Half of the total cost.
  • Additional guests – The charge for an additional guest is $35.00US + VAT per adult & $25.00US + VAT per child between ages of 2 – 10 years. However, for the all-inclusive packages, the cost for an additional adult will be $50.00 + VAT and child (2- 10) $35.00US + VAT.
  • Should a reservation need to be cancelled, the following fees will apply to the value of the first night’s accommodation for each room booked: No cancellation fee, if cancelled less than 72 hours prior to intended arrival. 50% cancellation fee, if cancelled less than 48 hours prior to intended arrival. 100% cancellation fee, if cancelled less than 24 hours prior to intended arrival.
  • No pets allowed.
  • No more than 2 adults and 1 child will be permitted to stay in a room except for family rooms where 4 persons can occupy.
  • All guests are required to secure their personal belongings as management will not be responsible for lost items, however Hotel security will be on duty 24/7.
  • All government taxes and V.A.T will be charged to bills.
  • Rates are subject to change depending on seasons and country or company festival.
  • Standard check-in time is 2:00pm and check –out time is 12:00 NOON
  • Valid identification card is required when making bookings.
Fun Park Rules:
  • No swimming is permitted at night. Please note that non swimming zones are dangerous as they have steep drops.
  • No nudity or semi nude swim wear is allowed.
  • No lewd behavior will be tolerated.
  • No cans, glass bottles or glass wear will be accepted into the Park.
  • No pets are allowed into the Park.
