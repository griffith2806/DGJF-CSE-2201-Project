Private & Special Events

Whether you are an individual or private company you can host your special event at Splashmins Resort. Here we have a variety of settings to offer you.

If you choose to host your event we can set up a section for you either at the Eco Park or Water & Fun Park. You can use our upper open view Banquet Facility equipped with private bar, washrooms, dance floor and space to accommodate up to 200 persons.

There is also our Conference room which can house 70 persons or if you need a more relaxing environment we can set up a corner for you within the confines of the Resort.

It is also possible for you to rent our entire Resort for your event. The choice is yours as your possibilities are endless when you choose us.

Some of the events that can be hosted here...

  • Children/ Adult Birthday Parties
  • Anniversaries
  • Graduations
  • Balls
  • Farewells
  • Company Fun days
  • Retreats
  • Weddings
  • Reunions
  • Bachelors Parties etc
