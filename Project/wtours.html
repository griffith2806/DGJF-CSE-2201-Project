Wetland Tours

Our Wetland Tour is a 45 minutes trip by boat down into Guyana’s wetlands savannahs. These tours leave from within the resort and take you on an adventurous journey. Enjoy nature at its best while you experience some of Guyana’s rare birds, and monkeys who jump from tree to tree.

A 15 person tour is only $7000GY or $36.00US.
