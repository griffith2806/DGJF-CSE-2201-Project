About Camping Grounds

Indulge in nature and experience Guyanese lifestyle to the fullest. Pick fruits, climb trees, bush cook or BBQ; it’s all a real experience.

Splashmins Eco Adventure Park and Camping Grounds is located on the opposite side of the Fun Park just a mere two minutes boat ride away. It is accessible from the Linden Soesdyke highway; only about 1 minute drive, South from the Fun Park’s main entrance.

Relaxing in hammocks tied between any of our fruit trees or camping out in the open between 25 acres of lush vegetation are all you’ll need to bring back to the grass roots of living in Guyana.
