V.I.P Platinum Cards

These cards are recommended for business persons and Companies. These cards will cost $100,000 per year and will have the following benefits attached:

  • Entrance for (6) on any day of the year including event days
  • Entrance for (6) to New Eco Adventure Camping Grounds
  • 10% Kayaks, Floats and Peddle boat rides
  • 15% Hotel discount on personal, friends, family or associates bookings
  • 15% Discount on Wetland Tours and motorized rides
  • Extended credit facility by application

Family Gold Cards

These cards are recommended for families not exceeding five persons. These cards will cost $50,000 per year and will have the following benefits attached:

  • Entrance for family of Five (5) on any day of the year including event days
  • Entrance for (5) to New Eco Adventure Camping grounds
  • 10% peddle boat, kayak and float rides
  • Extended credit facility by application

Couples Silver Cards

These cards are recommended for couples. These cards will cost $15,000 per year and will have the following benefits attached:

  • Entrance for 2 persons to Splashmins on any day including event days
  • Entrance for 2 to New Eco Adventure Camping grounds
  • 5 % Peddle boat, float and kayak rides
  • 10% discount on all Hotel packages
