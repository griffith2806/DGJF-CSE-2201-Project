Water & Fun Park

Our water and Fun Park was one of the initial developments that attracted thousands to our resort. The side of the resort features a swimming area along our white sandy beaches where the entire family can relax and have fun.

Enjoy peddle boats & jet boats while there, or the children’s playground is a fine leisure for them while you make some time for yourself.

This side of the resort also features a gift shop, dozens of benabs, swingers and log benches for your seating pleasure, a beach side bar and restaurant, an open banquet hall for special events with great overhead scenery of the resort, conference hall which can accommodate some 70 persons and numerous games facilities such as pools and table tennis, basketball court and football field as well as volley ball nets.

Our luxury Hotels are also located just beyond our football field. Also from our Water and Fun Park access the wetland savannahs and our Eco camping grounds.

Come and enjoy the best Guyana has to offer at Splashmins water and Fun Park.
